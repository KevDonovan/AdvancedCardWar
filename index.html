Player Hand

How to play

Click the "Deal" button to begin. Black cards have a positive value and red cards a negative. The player wins by either hitting the number 21 and loses if the house reaches -21. Additionally, the player will win if the deck has run out of cards and the score is above 0 and less than 33, or less than -33. Otherwise, the house wins.
